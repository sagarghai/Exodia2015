CANSAT DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM

1. INTRODUCTION

Opencube Labs' CanSat Development Program is designed to give the experience of small scale space missions to every student in India. At the end of this 2-day workshop, we guarantee that each of the participants will become an expert in CanSat oriented project and research works and you could independently conduct the future CanSat missions for your experiments.

2. ABOUT OPENCUBE LABS'

Our Mission

Quoting Dalai Lama (1357-1419), The High Lama of Tibetan Buddhism “Share your knowledge. Its a way to achieve immortality”. Opencube Labs wishes to achieve said immortality by making the knowledge on advances in space technologies available to all the citizens of the global community. The company strives towards this goal by reaching the millions of people through workshops, seminars, educational labs and by building ingenious products to better facilitate learning and nurture ingenuity.

Our Vision

Opencube Labs believe that they are the beacons of light in this world of digital darkness. In a world where the knowledge to build intriguing and exciting new Space Technologies is highly monopolised by a few governing agencies, Opencube Labs intends to bridge the gap between the said agencies and the country's eager and talented youth so as to make knowledge accessible to all.

3. CANSAT DEVELOPMENT KIT

Opencube Labs' CanSat Development kit consists of :

  • CanSat development board
  • Onboard computer – Atmega8
  • Attitude determination system – Accelero plus gyro module
  • Communication system – RF transreciever module
  • Micro SD data logging unit
  • Payloads – Light and temperature
  • Power unit – 9V batteries
  • Descent control system – Plastic parachute and thread
  • CanSat structure
  • Gloves
  • Knife and scissors
  • Insulation tape and double sided tape
  • Multimeter
  • Resistor Box
  • Capacitor box
  • Wires
  • Breadboard
  • Glue gun
  • Alluminium foil
  • Launch unit – CPR-600 sounding balloon and Helium gas
  • Flight software – Arduino IDE
  • Opencube Labs' CanSat development guide – Ebook

4. WORKSHOP DETAILS

  • Participants are grouped in a team of 3-5 during the course of the workshop.
  • In a team at least one person should have a laptop.
  • Kits are taken back after the workshop.
  • Interested participants can buy the kit for Rs. 6000/
  • Registration fees/participant : Rs. 1250/

AVIAN AEROSPACE

1.INTRODUCTION

Avian Aerospace with its team of Unmanned Systems Experts in association with Exodia 2015 organized at IIT Mandi, Himachal Pradesh in April 2015 brings you aerobotix’15 – UAV training program cum competition. This event is aimed at bringing awareness about unmanned systems and model rocketry, its importance and applications amongst the Indian students. The workshops is suitable for students from various engineering disciplines like Aero, Auto, Mech, EEE, ECE, E&I etc.., The students will participate in the training program in their college where the top performing teams will be selected to participate grand finale to be held during Exodia 2015, IIT Mandi.

  • Zonal Workshop cum prelims on different categories at colleges
  • Selection of Finalists from each zonal centre
  • Selected finalists from each zonal centre will be given instructions about the finals
  • Students compete at grand finale during Exodia 2015, IIT Mandi
  • Winners Selected

2. AEROBOTIX DEVELOPMENT KIT

  • Flapping wing MAV
  • Fixed Wing MAV
  • Single stage water rocketry
  • Electric Control line
  • Fixed wing UAV
  • Unmanned Amphibious vehicle
  • Unmanned water surface vehicle
  • Tricopter VTOL
  • Quad copter VTOL
  • Hydraulic Robot Arm

ROBOINDIA TECHNOLOGIES

1. INTRODUCTION

RoboIndia Technologies aims at encouraging young minds to have a wide area of vision in the field of by motivating / encouraging youths to actively participate in the various States and Inter States Level Technical Symposiums, Competitions, Trainings.

2. EVENT DETAILS

A States level championship and workshop series jointed by ‘Exodia’ IIT-MANDI and RoboIndia Technologies, This Year Prelims Rounds of ‘Exodia’ will be held at Selective Zonal Centers (Colleges/Universities) all across India. This is a 2 Stage Event as detailed below:

Stage 1 (WORKSHOP ROUND to be held at college):

  • Workshop will be held at different centers all across India.
  • All the participants who want to participate in Competition are required to attend the workshops at any Center.
  • The workshop fee includes the Registration charges, Kit, Tutorial and the certification.
  • The Duration of the Workshop will be of 2 Days(7-8 hours each day)

Stage 2 (competition to be held at College on the second day of workshop):

  • Just after the workshop a Mega Competition will be held.
  • Winners will be awarded by Certificate of Merit, and will be recognized as Zonal Final winner of States Level championship.
  • A Certificate of Excellence will also be awarded to the Winning Team.
  • Top 12 students (3 Teams, group of 4 members) will selected by us which will go for final round in IIT- Mandi.
  • In Final Round 1st Winning Prize 20000/- Rs.

3. WORKSHOP HIGHLIGHTS :

  • Receive knowledge from corporate peoples with personal one-on-one attention.
  • Learn and interact with one of the respected authorities.
  • RoboIndia Technologies certification programs are recognized in the industry.
  • Improve your job prospects and get an edge over your counterparts.
  • Interactive Query sessions, Live Demos, PowerPoint Presentation.
